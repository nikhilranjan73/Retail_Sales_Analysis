# Retail_Store_Sales_Analysis


## STORE BACKGROUND
•	A retail store chain tracks daily sales transactions, including order details, customer info, product categories, order time, and order status.
•	The business wants to optimize operations, improve customer experience, and increase profitability using data-driven decisions.


### PROBLEMS Statement
The store does not have a clear idea about
•	Which product sell the most 
•	Customers preferences 
•	Which item brings the most profit
•	Where things are going wrong in the delivery or operations
Because of this, they are missing chances to earn more, losing customers, and taking poor business decisions

#### SOLUTION 
They need proper and simple insight to understand their sales, customers, and product performance better.
